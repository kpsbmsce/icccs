International Conference on Communications and Computer Science-2024
(ICCCS-2024)

22nd – 24th May, 2024

About Conference

The first International Conference on Communications and Computer Science (ICCCS-2024). ICCCS-2024 is organized by the Department of Computer Applications and the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at B. M. S. College of Engineering. It is planned to be hosted at the BMSCE Campus in Bengaluru from 22nd to 24th of May 2024. The conference aspires to provide a platform for researchers across the world to exchange their latest research findings and network with eminent speakers from academia, industry, and fellow researchers. The three days of the conference aim to provide hands-on tutorial sessions, and keynote sessions from eminent speakers focusing on the thrust areas related to the domain of the conference. This conference is focusing on providing a bird’s eye view of the research in communications and computer science.
